Red Square Infosys was born from a vision to bridge the gap between technology and human-centric design. Starting with a small team of passionate innovators, we laid the foundation for what would become a transformative journey in enterprise technology.

We delivered our first major enterprise solution, marking our entry into the world of large-scale digital transformation. This milestone validated our approach and opened doors to bigger opportunities in the corporate sector.
