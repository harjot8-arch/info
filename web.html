Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mobile and web development services.

What is the difference between a web app and a native app? +
A web app runs in a browser and is accessed via a URL, while a native app is built for a specific mobile operating system (iOS or Android) and is downloaded from an app store. Native apps can access device features like the camera or GPS more directly.
How long does a typical development project take? +
Project timelines vary greatly depending on the complexity, features, and scope. A small-scale website may take a few weeks, while a complex custom mobile app could take several months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.
Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance? +
Yes, we believe in long-term partnerships. We offer flexible support packages that include bug fixes, performance monitoring, security updates, and feature enhancements to ensure your application remains cutting-edge.
What is responsive design and why is it important? +
Responsive design is an approach to web design that makes web pages render well on a variety of devices and screen sizes. It's crucial for providing a consistent and user-friendly experience to all visitors, regardless of whether they are on a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.
